Albondiga Soup!

Description

Albondigas are meatballs made out of meat, veggies and rice. Today we will be making turkey albondigas just how My mom makes them. Albondigas are always good, especially after a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 8 cups of chicken or beef stock
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 pound of green beans, ends chopped
  • 2 large carrots peeled and sliced
  • 1/3 cup of white rice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh spearming leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp of dried oregano
  • cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. To make the soup base, first heat an large pot with oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for a minute more.
  2. In a small bowl mix uncooked rice, meat, spearming leaves, parsley, salt, and pepper. thoroughly mix and then crack raw egg. Mix again then form mixture into 1-inch meatballs
  3. add meatballs to the simmering soup. Cover and let simmer for 1/2 hour. Add the peas towards the end of cooking. Add a few pinches of oregano and sprinkle with salt and pepper, and some cayenne, to taste.
  4. Ladle into bowls and garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.
