Tacos De Asada!!

Description

This is a classic mexican recipe for some tacos de asada. All tacos are good in my opinion, whether its tacos de Pescado (fish), Al pastor(pork), or Pollo(chicken), but nothing beats good ol asada. Adding classic toppings like chopped cilantro, onion, and your salsa of choice makes it a simple yet delciious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. of Skirt Steak
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped cilantro
  • Salsa of choice
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • limes
  • 1 tsp of ground cumin
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • i tsp of dried oregano

Steps

  1. Chop the skirt steak into small chunks and then put it in a medium mixing bowl
  2. Add grated garlic cloves, ground cumin, chili powder, and dried oregano. Then mix it all together!
  3. Next, heat up a skillet to medium-high heat and add the 2 tbsp of vegetable oil. Once the skillet is hot add the steak, and cook, stirring often, until the steak has browned or until desired cookness.
  4. While the asada cooks, grab another skillet and warm it over medium heat and heat each tortilla for about 20 seconds
  5. After everything is done, fill tortillas with filling and add chopped onion, cilantro, choice of slasa, and a squeeze of lime
  6. Enjoy!
