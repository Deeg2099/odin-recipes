Enchiladas Verdes

Description

Enchiladas are are another one of my family's food, Enchiladas require a bit more effort because you have to make the green slasa that goes with it but trust me, it is worth it.

Ingredients

  • Canola Oil
  • 1 onion
  • minced garlic
  • Green tomatillos
  • jalapenos
  • crema
  • chicken stock
  • cilantro
  • chicken breast cooked and shredded (rotisserie chicken works best)
  • corn tortillas
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin for taste

Steps

  1. Heat the shredded chicken in a frying pan with the seasonings mentioned above, or preferreed seasonings
  2. Add 1/4 cup of chicken stock and let it cook for 5 minutes
  3. In a pot, add water and salt to taste, bring to a boil. Once boiling, add tomatillos, garlic, onion, and jalapenos for about 8 minutes.
  4. Next, drain pot and add ingredients, crema, and 1/4 chicken stock into a blender to blend
  5. Once ingredients are blended, pour into a hot frying pan with a little bit of oil and let it cook for about 5 minutes
  6. Heat tortillas on the stove top, then cover the tortillas in the green salsa
  7. Fill the tortillas with the chicken filling and roll them
  8. Place the rolled tortillas in a baking dish and cover with remaining salsa and mozzarella cheese
  9. Bake at 400 degrees fahrenheit for 15- 20 minutes or until cheese is melted
  10. Enjoy!
